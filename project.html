Midnight Drift

Location: Tokyo, JP Client: RWB Role: Lead Photographer

A high-octane visual exploration of underground car culture. Shot in the heart of Tokyo during the twilight hours, this project focuses on the raw energy, speed, and aggressive styling of modified street cars. The aesthetic leans heavily on neon reflections, deep shadows, and cinematic motion blur to capture the feeling of the drive.
